Ranking Rules! 2026 👑
Official Guidelines
🧐
How does Ranking Work?
To rank up, PvP someone on the list. First to 10 kills wins. Winner travels to the opponent's region for the next round. Must record 5 clips of POV and screenshot enchantments.
✅
Allowed
Mounts, Pets, Talents, Runes, and playing on shared accounts are all permitted.
⛔
Strictly Forbidden
Hacks, plugins, panels, and spectators. Emulator vs Mobile matches are not allowed.
🤔
Disputes & Reporting
Report opponents who don't record or "duck" matches for 3+ days. Bans range from 7 days to 1 month.
