Ranking Rules 2026 👑
Official Guidelines
🧐
How does Ranking Work?
Challenge someone on the list to a First to 10 kills match. Winner plays in the opponent's region next round. You must record 5 clips of POV and screenshot your enchantments before the match.
✅
Allowed
Mounts Pets Talents Runes Shared Accounts are all permitted in ranked matches.
⛔
Strictly Forbidden
Hacks Plugins Panels Spectators are banned. Emulator vs Mobile matches are not allowed.
🤔
Disputes & Reporting
Report opponents who refuse to record or duck matches for 3+ days. Bans range from 7 days to 1 month depending on severity.
